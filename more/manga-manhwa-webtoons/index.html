Manga, Manhwa, and Webtoons list

A collection of manga, manhwa, webtoons that I’ve read. Everything under “2023” and earlier are for English-only titles.

Kudos to:

  • Dr. Emily Hall for putting print manhwa on my list, and for recommending Abandoned Empress, Remarried Empress, Orange Marmalade, and all of Carnby Kim’s work.
  • Rosa, Orian, Jaune, and Leo for sharing their interest in manhwa and webtoons with me.
  • Ki Cafe for their semi-public manga collection. This was a big help in helping me evaluate whether a manga is worth reading without having to twiddle my thumbs with the public libraries.
  • The Vancouver Public Library for their public manga collection.
