Special interfaith holy water

Special thanks to Jeff and Wendy for testing the corresponding proof of concept.

Ingredients

  • 8-10 butterfly pea leaves
  • 60 mL hot water
  • 180 mL cold water + 30 mL lemon juice
  • 30-45 mL of some sort of blue drink (I used Kool-Aid blue raspberry jammers)
  • Dash of pearl shimmer powder (optional)
  • Two spoonfuls of your favourite bubble tea ingredients, Gushers, or chopped gummies

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, steep the butterfly pea leaves in the hot water for five minutes. Chill that in the fridge for 5 minutes.
  2. Add your favourite bubble tea ingredients in a glass jar (or cup)
  3. Pour in your blue drink into the glass jar
  4. Combine your lemon mixture (lemon juice or lemonade) with the butterfly tea. The lemon’s acidity will turn the tea a pinkish purple (and is normal). Pour that into the jar
  5. Add ice and pearl shimmer powder (if using), stir, and serve!

Notes

  • Most bubble tea ingredients can only be bought in wholesale sizes. If you have friends who love drinking bubble tea, you could pool in some money to buy a pack. Or if you frequent a local bubble tea shop, you could ask them if they can sell you the toppings
  • You can use bottled lemonade instead of lemon juice and water, but you may have to dilute it so it’s less sweet.
  • You can also use sparkling water instead of cold water
  • Buy pearl shimmer powder from a place selling cake decorating ingredients such as Michaels, Bulk Barn, or some gourmet store (such as Gourmet Warehouse in BC)
