Exorcism sorbet

Ice recipe adapted from Food dot com’s Italian Ice recipe, with the syrup recipe adapted from FIVEheartHOME’s dye-free fruit juice snow cone syrup recipe.

Italian ice

Ingredients

  • 500 mL warm water
  • 75g (93 mL) sugar
  • 125 mL lemon juice
  • 2.5 mL lemon extract or 5 mL lemon zest

Steps

  1. In a sauce pan, dissolve sugar in water
  2. Add lemon juice.
  3. Pour mixture into a baking tray (I used a loaf tin) and freeze, stirring every 30-45 minutes

Syrups

For each colour…

Ingredients

  • 125 mL your favourite beverage in the colour you want (I used Kool Aid Berry Jammers for blue)
  • 60-125 mL (50-100g) sugar

Steps

  1. Over medium heat in a small pot, bring drink and sugar to a boil.
  2. Turn to low and simmer for 5 minutes, stirring until the sugar dissolves.
  3. Pour syrup in a container and cool completely.
