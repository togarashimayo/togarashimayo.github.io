# Porridge made from grain that was just lying around

Recipe adapted from [Made With Lau's chicken congee recipe](https://www.madewithlau.com/recipes/chicken-congee)

## Ingredients

- 200 grams Pearl barley
- 1000-1750 mL water
- 15 mL chicken bouillon
- Salt to taste
- Rehydrated wakame
- Crab
- Orange fish roe (optional)

## Steps

1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil
2. Wash and rinse the barley.
3. Once the water boils, add the barley and reduce heat to medium hear
4. Let simmer for 30-60 minutes.
5. Stir in the chicken broth and salt.
6. Top the porridge with 15 mL of rehydrated seaweed, then the crab, and finally the flying fish roe (if using any)

## Notes

- The official manga uses a 1:5 grain:water ratio which makes for a very thick porridge. If you want a thinner porridge (akin to congee), use more water.
- Get fish roe and crab from an Asian supermarket.
- I used imitation crab instead of actual crab.
