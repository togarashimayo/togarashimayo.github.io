# Poffins (Pokemon Diamond, Pearl, Platinum, and Go)

# Ingredients
* [Chilled shortbread dough](../shortbread)
* Jimmies in your favourite colour

# Steps
1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
2. Portion out the shortbread dough to 25 grams before shaping them into American footballs shaped patties. Place each cookie on a parchment paper lined tray.
3. Press the jimmies on the shortbread patties.
4. Chill for 45 minutes.
5. Bake for 13-15 minutes.
6. Let cool on the tray for 1-2 minutes, before transferring to a wire rack and letting cool for 5-10 minutes.

# Notes
* 13 minutes will get you a soft yet crumbly cookie dough.
