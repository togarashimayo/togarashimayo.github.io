# Ink Pasta (Paper Mario: Thousand Year Door)

## Ingredients
* 1 ~400-450 lb squid ink pasta
* 9 cloves garlic, finely minced or grated
* 60 mL cup olive oil
* Salt and pepper to taste
* Parmesan cheese (optional)

## Steps

1. Boil pasta according to package instructions. Save 120 mL of the pasta water.
2. In a large pan over medium heat, heat olive oil until shimmering.
3. Add garlic and sautee for 3-5 minutes.
4. Gradually add in the pasta and pasta water, before stirring until combined.
5. Serve immediately with parmesan cheese.


## Notes
* Most specialty grocery stores should sell squid ink pasta. I got mine from Gourmet Warehouse.
* If you accidentally emptied your pasta water, you can make a subsitute using by microwaving 120 mL of pasta water and a sprinkle of cornstarch for 1 minute.
