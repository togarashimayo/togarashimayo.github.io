# Eggs in a hole

## Ingredients for one serving

* 1 egg
* 1 slice of bread
* 1-2 large sausages
* Salt and pepper to taste

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
2. On a parchment lined paper, tray sausages while leaving room for your bread.
3. Cook sausages on one side for 15-20 minutes.
4. In the meantime, cut a hole in the bread using a cookie cutter or a knife.
5. Take the tray out of the oven, flip the sausages around, and tray the bread on the sheet.
6. Crack the egg into the bread and season with salt and pepper.
7. Return the tray to the oven and bake for 8-10 minutes.
